About Techwich

Techwich is a technology education company founded by Rasool Seyghaly and Eisa Goltapeh on 26th March 2020. Our mission is to make science, technology, engineering, and math accessible and engaging for everyone by offering bite-sized, interactive learning experiences that are designed and taught by experienced industry professionals.
